Security

Enterprise-Grade Security, On Your Terms

Every layer of MedRecords AI is designed with security as the default, not an add-on.

🔐

AES-256 Encryption at Rest

All stored data encrypted with AES-256, the same standard used by banks and government agencies. Your records are unreadable without your encryption keys.

🔒

TLS 1.3 in Transit

When using cloud AI, data is encrypted with TLS 1.3 — the latest transport layer security protocol. Data is processed and immediately discarded. Never stored.

📋

HIPAA-Compliant Audit Trail

HMAC-signed audit entries with tamper-evident logging. Every access, every action, every export is recorded in a cryptographically verifiable log you control.

👥

Role-Based Access Control

Session-based authentication with rate limiting and CSRF protection. Control who can access, process, and export records within your organization.

🔌

Offline License Validation

License validates without internet. No phone home. No usage reporting. Your air-gapped deployment stays air-gapped — even for license management.

🚫

No Telemetry

Zero usage tracking. Zero analytics. Zero data collection. MedRecords AI does not know how you use it, what you process, or how many cases you run.
