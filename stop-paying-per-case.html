Frequently Asked Questions

You receive a perpetual license key that activates the software on your machine. The license is yours forever — there is no expiration date. You download the installer, enter your key, and you are up and running. All future updates within your major version are included at no extra cost.
The license itself is a one-time payment with no recurring charges. There are two optional ongoing costs: (1) Priority Support at $1,000/year, which includes guaranteed response times, phone access, and all version upgrades (Pro license includes the first year free), and (2) API costs if you use Claude for AI processing, typically $1–$2 per case, billed directly by Anthropic. You can also use free local AI models via Ollama to eliminate API costs entirely.
Core ($3,997) includes AI medical summarization (3 modes), interactive timeline and chronology, treatment gap detection, source page linking, billing extraction, agentic alerts, AI chat over records, and HIPAA-compliant audit trail. Pro ($7,997) adds demand package generation (DOCX and XLSX), case valuation engine, negligence and standard of care detection, auto-task generation for paralegals, AI injury body map visualization, smoking gun detection, and priority support.
Each license covers one installation. Additional seats are available at a per-seat discount. For firms needing five or more seats, contact us for volume pricing with unlimited seats for larger deployments. All seats share the same perpetual license terms.
If you choose to use Claude (by Anthropic) as your AI backend, API usage is billed directly by Anthropic to your account — not through us. The average cost is approximately $1–$2 per case, depending on record length and processing mode. You can also run entirely free local AI models via Ollama, which means zero ongoing API costs. Many firms use Claude for complex cases and Ollama for routine ones.
