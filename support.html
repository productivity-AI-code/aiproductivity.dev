Upload your document via the Files tab, select a processing mode (Fast, Auto, or Thorough), and click Summarize. MedRecords AI will extract, analyze, and organize the medical records into a structured summary with timeline, source linking, and gap detection.
MedRecords AI supports PDF (both native text and scanned documents via OCR), TIFF, PNG, JPG, DOCX, TXT, CSV, and XML. Scanned PDFs and image files are automatically processed through the built-in OCR engine before analysis.
Fast (1–2 min) provides a quick overview suitable for initial case screening. Auto (2–5 min) offers a balanced analysis with timeline and gap detection. Thorough (5–15 min) performs deep analysis with detailed findings, cross-referencing, and comprehensive source linking — ideal for demand packages and trial preparation.
